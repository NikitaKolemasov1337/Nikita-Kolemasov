The real story of Ron Woodroof, a Texas electrician who was diagnosed with AIDS in 1986. Then the doctors gave him only six months, but he did not want to come to terms with the death sentence and managed to prolong his life by taking non-traditional drugs, and then established an underground and profitable business selling them to other patients.
