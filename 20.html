The two best graduates of the police academy were on opposite sides of the barricade: one of them was a mafia agent in the ranks of law enforcement agencies, the other was a "mole" embedded in the mafia. Everyone considers it their duty to find and destroy the enemy, but the constant life in distorted realities changes the inner world of the heroes.
