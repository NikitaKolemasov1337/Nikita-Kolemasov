One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest

Description
Directed by Miloš Forman.
Release 11/19/1975. Lease

Faking insanity in the hope of avoiding imprisonment, Randall Patrick McMurphy ends up in a psychiatric clinic, where the cruel sister Mildred Ratched is almost undivided master. McMurphy is amazed that other patients have come to terms with the existing state of affairs, and some even deliberately came to the hospital, hiding from the frightening outside world. And he decides to rebel. By oneself.
